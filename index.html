THE GOAL: VERTICAL POWER
This session trains fast-twitch muscle fibers, explosive hip extension, and reactive strength — the exact qualities that add inches to your vertical. Low reps, full effort, full recovery. Speed of movement matters more than the weight on the bar.
🔥 Warm-Up (8 min): 3 min light bike or jog → ankle circles ×15 each → leg swings (front/back + side) ×12 each → bodyweight jump squats ×10 (easy) → glute bridges ×15 → hip flexor stretch 30 sec each side.
STRENGTH BASE
2 ex · Heavy · Low Reps
⚡
Why heavy strength first? Building maximal strength is the foundation — you can only express power up to your strength ceiling. These sets build the base everything else sits on.

⚡
Speed of Intent
Always try to move the weight as fast as possible on the concentric. Even under load, that intent activates fast-twitch fibers.
😴
Rest Fully
Power work needs full recovery between sets. Cutting rest short turns this into cardio. 3 min on heavy sets.
📅
Frequency
Once per week is enough in-season. Off-season you can go twice weekly with 72+ hrs between sessions.
🏀
Practice Still Wins
The gym builds the engine. Jumping on the court — practicing approach jumps, two-foot jumps — teaches your body to USE that power.
